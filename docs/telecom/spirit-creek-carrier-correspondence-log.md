## Classification rules

- **Message sent** means an outbound inquiry was transmitted.
- **Ticket acknowledged** means a provider created a case; it is not acceptance or service confirmation.
- **Provider capability statement** describes general capabilities and does not prove inventory or eligibility for a specific Saskatchewan rate centre.
- **Quoted commercial offer** requires written prices and terms attributable to the provider.
- **Carrier acceptance** requires explicit written acceptance or onboarding approval.
- **Regulatory response** requires substantive guidance from the regulator or administrator.
- **Formal approval** must never be inferred from an acknowledgement, ticket number, draft, or internal readiness document.
