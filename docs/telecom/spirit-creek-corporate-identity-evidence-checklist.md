# Spirit Creek Communications — Corporate Identity Evidence Checklist

**Status:** Evidence intake required  
**Last updated:** 2026-07-20  
**Purpose:** Establish one authoritative, reviewable source for the legal applicant identity used in carrier, numbering, emergency-service, banking, tax, and regulatory workflows.

## Rules

- Do not infer or invent a legal name, corporation number, jurisdiction, registration date, operating-name relationship, address, officer, or signing authority.
- Store only approved business evidence. Do not commit personal identity documents, credentials, payment data, private account links, or unrelated personal information.
- Record the source document, issue date, issuing authority, review date, reviewer, and any expiry or supersession relationship.
- Until documentary evidence is reviewed, external-facing records must use: `Spirit Creek Communications — exact legal entity and operating-name relationship pending documentary verification`.

## Intake procedure

1. Place candidate evidence in the approved restricted document location.
2. Verify that the issuing authority, entity name, identifier, and date are legible.
3. Compare the legal name character-for-character across all documents.
4. Record discrepancies without resolving them by assumption.
5. Create a sanitized evidence reference containing document title, issuer, date, and restricted storage location.
6. Update carrier, numbering, E911, and regulatory drafts only after the identity fields are verified.
7. Obtain explicit review before any signature, certification, submission, or legal representation.

## Completion criteria

This checklist is complete only when the exact legal name, corporation number, jurisdiction, operating-name relationship, and signing authority are supported by current documentary evidence and reconciled across all active application drafts.
