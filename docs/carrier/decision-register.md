## Decision template

When adding a decision, record:

- date and approver;
- alternatives considered;
- legal and regulatory assumptions;
- customer and public-safety effects;
- security and resilience effects;
- cost and dependency implications;
- implementation owner;
- review or expiry date;
- linked evidence and superseding decision, if any.
