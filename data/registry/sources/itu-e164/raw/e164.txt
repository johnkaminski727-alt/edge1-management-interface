Note from TSB
1.
This List of ITU-T Recommendation E.164 assigned country codes replaces the previous one
published as Annex to the ITU Operational Bulletin No. 982 of 15 June 2011. Since then, various
new assignments have been made, and they have been published in the ITU Operational Bulletin
up to No. 991 of 1 November 2011.
2.

This List includes:
- a list of ITU-T Recommendation E.164 assigned country codes - numerical order;
- a list of ITU-T Recommendation E.164 assigned country codes - alphabetical order.

3.
This List will be updated by numbered series of amendments published in the ITU
Operational Bulletin. Furthermore, the information contained in this Annex is also available on the
ITU website www.itu.int/itu-t/bulletin/annex.html .
4.

country or geographical area, or of its authorities.
